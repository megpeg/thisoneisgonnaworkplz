    How to Make Spam Musubi

    Now that you know there is only three ingredients needed to make this yummy snack, I will now walk you through how to put it all together!!

    Step 1: Cook the rice

    Step 2: Pan-fry the spam (if you would like add teriaki sauce for flavor!!)

    Step 3: Place the seaweed, shiny side down on the counter

    Step 4: Build the spam musubi in a musubi mold (if you dont have a mold, you can use the spam can)

    Step 5: Remove the mold

    Step 6: Repeat & serve!!

    Step 6: Wrap the spam musubi

    Enjoy your tasty musubi !!
